ABOUT US

Welcome to IMS, Our Inventory Management System is designed to streamline inventory processes, ensuring accuracy, productivity, and real-time data integration. Built with cutting-edge technologies, our platform leverages the power of Oracle Database and is crafted using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and EJS to deliver a seamless user experience.

Technologies Implemented:
At the core of our system is the robust Oracle Database, providing unparalleled data security, reliability, and scalability. Our web application is developed with modern web technologies, ensuring a responsive, intuitive, and user-friendly interface. The combination of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and EJS enables us to offer a dynamic and efficient inventory management solution tailored to meet your specific needs.

Key Features:
    • Customer Master: Maintain comprehensive records of all your customers, including contact details, transaction history, and preferences.
    • Item Master: Keep detailed information about all your inventory items, from stock levels to specifications and supplier information.
    • Supplies Master: Manage your suppliers effectively, track orders, and ensure timely restocking of essential items.
    • Tax Master: Simplify tax management with customizable tax settings and automated calculations.
    • QR Code Technology: Our innovative QR code feature allows for quick and easy data integration. Generate QR codes for items, customers, and transactions, enhancing productivity and accuracy. Scan QR codes to update or retrieve data directly from the Oracle Database.
